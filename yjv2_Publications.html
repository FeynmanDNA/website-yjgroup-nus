String Search Function

To search through our 90+ publications, simply type in the search box below anything from keywords in the title, name of author or journal, or year of publication.
The author is displayed as First Name (single letter abbreviation) concatonated with Middle Name (if any, also in single letter abbreviation), and followed by the full Surname. For example, Lu Chen's author display will be "C Lu", Ouyang Zhong-Can's author name will be shown as "ZC Ouyang", and Michael P Sheetz will be "MP Sheetz".

The search is case insensitive, and we have normalized the characters in the publication information during search. Accents or diacritics are removed. So if you want to search for "P Dröge", just typing in "P Droge"; the same goes for "Poincare".
